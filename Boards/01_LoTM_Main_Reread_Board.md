# Archive Investigations

## Why Did Klein Hire Sharron?

### Evidence Chain

Ambassador Crisis
→ Need for protection
→ Kaspars
→ Maric
→ Divination
→ Sharron protection begins

### Conclusion

Klein did not hire Sharron because he trusted her.

He hired her because:

* He needed protection.
* Maric vouched for her.
* Divination indicated acceptable risk.
* Survival outweighed uncertainty.

## Why Did The Tarot Club Become Recurring?

### Evidence Chain

Gray Fog accident
→ First gathering
→ Audrey requests continuation
→ Alger supports usefulness
→ Klein evaluates benefits
→ Recurring gatherings established

### Conclusion

The recurring Tarot Club emerged from:

* Audrey's desire to continue meeting.

* Klein's realization that the information value outweighed the risk.
