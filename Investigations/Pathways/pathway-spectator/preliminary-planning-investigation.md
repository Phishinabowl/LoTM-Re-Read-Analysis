## Evidence

### Check 1: Primary Pathway Name

#### Chapter 179

- What happened: Audrey asks whether the near-extinction of the Rainbow Salamander means that the Spectator pathway will be severed.
- Why it matters: This is the first exact `Spectator pathway` phrasing found in the sweep and gives an early reader-safe pathway label.

### Check 2: Dragon Alias

#### Chapter 237

- What happened: Audrey remembers that Derrick called the Spectator pathway the Dragon pathway and tied it to the Dragon race.
- Why it matters: Dragon is not just random creature flavor; it is an explicit alternate pathway name from City of Silver / ancient-history framing.

#### Chapter 238

- What happened: Audrey reacts to the dragon-history material by thinking of the future of the Spectator pathway and wanting to call it the Dragon pathway.
- Why it matters: Preserve Dragon as a reader-visible alias, but keep it distinct from the primary modern pathway slug.

### Check 3: Visionary Reveal

#### Chapter 485

- What happened: Roselle's diary states that Sequence 0 of the Spectator pathway is Visionary, with Sequence 1 Author and Sequence 2 Discerner also exposed.
- Why it matters: Visionary is confirmed as the Sequence 0 / formal endpoint, not the primary slug for the pathway at this stage.

#### Chapter 486

- What happened: Klein uses `Visionary pathway` while connecting the Dragon of Imagination / Ankewelt material to the Creator and the Spectator cluster.
- Why it matters: Exact `Visionary pathway` usage exists, but it is rare and later than the dominant `Spectator pathway` usage.

## Key Reveal Notes To Preserve

- Preserve staged reader naming.
- Chapter 179 gives the first exact `Spectator pathway` phrasing.
- Chapters 237, 238, 291, and 386 establish `Dragon pathway` as a real early/City of Silver and ancient-dragon alias.
- Chapter 485 reveals Visionary as Sequence 0 of the Spectator pathway.
- Exact `Visionary pathway` usage remains sparse and late/formal.
- Keep `pathway-spectator.md` as the slug.
- Preserve Dragon as an early alias and Visionary as Sequence 0/later formal/artwork terminology.

## Conclusion

Use `pathway-spectator.md` as the canonical pathway page. Preserve `Dragon pathway` as an early/ancient alias and `Visionary` as the Sequence 0 / later formal / artwork label.
