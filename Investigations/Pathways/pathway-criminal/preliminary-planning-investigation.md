## Evidence

### Check 1: Modern Pathway Name

#### Chapter 258

- What happened: Klein identifies the Blood Sanctify Sect as possessing the Criminal pathway, also known as the Devil pathway.
- Why it matters: This is the cleanest modern in-text anchor for `Criminal pathway`, and it directly records `Devil pathway` as an alias rather than forcing a separate slug.

- What happened: The same passage begins the local sequence ladder with Sequence 9 Criminal and Sequence 8 Coldblooded / Unwinged Angel.
- Why it matters: Criminal is simultaneously the pathway label and a sequence-facing term, so the eventual page should keep sequence context separate from name-arbitration context.

### Check 2: Devil As Alias And Sequence/Race Term

#### Chapter 258

- What happened: `Devil pathway` appears as an alternate label in Klein's recalled information on Blood Sanctify Sect.
- Why it matters: Devil is reader-visible and common, but it is also too overloaded to be the clean slug.

#### Chapter 292

- What happened: Derrick explains that the Abyss pathway's Sequence 6 is called Devil and that this is the origin of the Devil race's name.
- Why it matters: Devil must be preserved carefully as pathway alias, Sequence 6 title, and race-origin terminology.

### Check 3: Abyss As Ancient/Formal Name

#### Chapter 292

- What happened: Klein infers that before the Cataclysm the Devil pathway was called the Abyss pathway, and wonders whether Sequence 0 might be Abyss.
- Why it matters: Abyss is important formal/ancient/artwork terminology, but the text frames it as older terminology discovered after the modern Criminal/Devil labels.

## Key Reveal Notes To Preserve

- Preserve staged reader naming.
- Chapter 258 introduces the Criminal pathway and says it is also known as the Devil pathway.
- Chapter 292 says the older/pre-Cataclysm name is Abyss pathway and clarifies Devil as Sequence 6 / race-origin terminology.
- Keep `pathway-criminal.md` as the slug.
- Preserve Devil as common alternate terminology while avoiding confusion with the Sequence 6 Devil title.
- Keep Mother Tree of Desire as the separate Outer Deity / Outer God pressure.

## Conclusion

Use `pathway-criminal.md` as the canonical pathway page. Preserve `Devil pathway` as a common alias and `Abyss pathway` as the ancient/formal/artwork label, with explicit notes separating Devil as Sequence 6 and race-origin terminology.
