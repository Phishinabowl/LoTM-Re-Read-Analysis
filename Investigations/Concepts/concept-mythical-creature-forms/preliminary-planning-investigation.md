## Scope

Track mythical creature forms as a shared concept/index rather than as one glossary page per form. The eventual concept page should explain the concept once and keep a table of known forms with links to the relevant pathway, character, deity, Uniqueness, and evidence records.

## Current Structural Decision

- Do not create `mythical-creature-*` pages by default.
- Keep specific form/state details on the relevant pathway and character pages.
- Use `concept-mythical-creature-forms.md` as the shared relationship-seed target when a form relationship needs to be graph-visible.
- Preserve the exact form name, form version/stage, and Sequence or advancement threshold in the local table row, data block, and relationship-seed notes.
