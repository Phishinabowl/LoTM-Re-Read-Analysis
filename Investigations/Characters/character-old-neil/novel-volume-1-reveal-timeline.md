What does the novel reader know about Old Neil through Chapter 34, especially his role inside the Tingen Nighthawks, his pathway status, and his early relationship to Klein?

Old Neil's early role as civilian staff, Mystery Pryer, Roselle diary interpreter contact, formula/material custodian, and Klein's first practical mysticism instructor.

Volume 1, Chapters 1-34. Current active review window: Chapters 19-34.

## Evidence

### Check 1: First Mention as Civilian Staff

#### Chapter 19

- Old Neil is named as one of the Tingen Nighthawks' civilian staff, along with Rozanne and Bredt.
- His listed duties fall under the team's ordinary operational infrastructure: visitors, room cleaning, case files, inventory registration, and guarding the armory, storeroom, and archives.
- Why it matters: Old Neil first enters the reader's model as part of the Nighthawks' support staff and custody apparatus, not yet as Klein's mentor.

### Check 2: First Direct Workplace Interaction

#### Chapters 20-21

- Dunn directs Klein to spend time with Old Neil after handling advance pay with Mrs. Orianna.
- Klein's visit to Old Neil's office becomes the first Roselle diary contact point and gives Klein a reason to recognize Roselle's writing as familiar.
- Why it matters: Old Neil becomes a practical gateway into the Nighthawks' knowledge work: documents, language puzzles, and unusual historical material.

### Check 3: Pathway Identity

#### Chapter 22

- Rozanne says Old Neil's Sequence 9 title is `Mystery Pryer`.
- She associates him with interesting magical rituals.
- Why it matters: Old Neil becomes the first named team member whose pathway identity is attached to mysticism knowledge rather than combat or command.

#### Chapter 28

- Dunn confirms that `Mystery Pryer` is the power Old Neil controls.
- Dunn explains that the Church's Mystery Pryer formula and some non-directly chained later Sequences came from the Moses Ascetic Order.
- Why it matters: Old Neil's pathway status becomes part of the Church's incomplete pathway-access model rather than a stray personal detail.

### Check 4: Formula/Material Custody and Seer Advancement

#### Chapter 31

- Dunn says he will have Old Neil retrieve the Seer formula and corresponding materials from Chanis Gate.
- Old Neil prepares Klein's Seer potion in the Nighthawks team's alchemy room.
- Why it matters: Old Neil is trusted inside the Nighthawks' formula/material workflow and becomes directly involved in Klein's advancement.

### Check 5: First Practical Instructor

#### Chapter 32

- Old Neil supervises Klein immediately after Klein consumes the Seer potion.
- He confirms Klein's successful advancement, congratulates him as a new Beyonder and Seer, and begins teaching Cogitation.
- He explains basic mysticism concepts such as Soul Body, Astral Projection, spirituality, and the practical relationship between Cogitation and Spirit Vision.
- Why it matters: Old Neil shifts from support staff and specialist into Klein's first direct mysticism instructor.

#### Chapter 33

- Old Neil teaches Klein how to switch Spirit Vision off and explains ritualistic magic, remnant spiritualities, and why potion ingredients remain spiritually active.
- He also clarifies that the headquarters beneath the Church of Evernight is protected Nighthawk space rather than a place where spirits and souls wander freely.
- Why it matters: Old Neil combines practical instruction, safety context, humor, and institutional knowledge into the early shape of Klein's training.

### Check 6: Chapter 34 Boundary

#### Chapter 34

- Klein uses Old Neil's aura-color explanations while observing the Tarot Club above the gray fog.
- Alger's explanation of Cogitation overlaps with Old Neil's earlier instruction but is not an Old Neil scene.
- Why it matters: Chapter 34 confirms that Old Neil's teaching has already become part of Klein's working interpretive toolkit, but it does not add a major new Old Neil disclosure.

## Interim Conclusions

### Character Role Through Chapter 34

Old Neil is introduced in layers:

1. Civilian staff inside the Tingen Nighthawks.
2. Custody/support worker tied to records, inventory, armory, storeroom, and archives.
3. Roselle diary contact and knowledge-work specialist.
4. Mystery Pryer with ritual/mysticism associations.
5. Trusted handler of formula/material access and potion preparation.
6. Klein's first practical instructor in Cogitation, Spirit Vision, and basic mysticism safety.

### Reader-State Boundary

Through Chapter 34, Old Neil is reader-safe as an eccentric, humorous, knowledgeable Nighthawks civilian staff member and Mystery Pryer who mentors Klein after his Seer advancement. Later tragedy, deeper personal history, and any later reinterpretation must remain excluded.
