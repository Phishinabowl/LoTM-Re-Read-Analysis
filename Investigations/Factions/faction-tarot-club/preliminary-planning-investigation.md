### Recruitment Order Cross-Check

### Nuance To Preserve

- Chapter 5 should not be treated as Audrey and Alger's first Tarot Club contact. It is Klein's tarot-symbolism seed; the gray-fog contact with Audrey and Alger is Chapter 6.
- Chapter 95 should not be treated as Derrick's own first awareness of the Tarot Club. It is Klein's first observation of Derrick's prayer. Derrick's direct contact begins in Chapter 137, his card is chosen in Chapter 138, and his first multi-member gathering is Chapter 143.
- Fors and Xio have long candidate/test arcs before they join. For future reader-boundary logic, distinguish `candidate discussed`, `personally contacted`, `card assigned`, and `first regular gathering`.
- Cattleya's join is unusually explicit: she asks whether she can participate and picks `The Hermit` in Chapter 564 before her first full regular gathering in Chapter 566.
- Leonard first builds a wrong/incomplete theory about a Fool-linked tarot organization in Chapter 850, but his actual informed join/card assignment is Chapter 951.
- Xio's exact private briefing/card-assignment scene remains less explicit than Leonard's. Chapter 957 shows her gray-fog cleansing and Klein already thinking of her as Miss Judgment; Chapter 958 confirms public introduction; Chapter 959 confirms Fors had already briefed her.
