# Goddess of Origins Preliminary Planning Investigation

## Related Glossary Thread

[Goddess of Origins](../../../Glossary_Threads/Deities/deity-ats-goddess-of-origins.md) planned.

## Question

How should the Goddess of Origins title cluster be represented without collapsing it into Earth Mother / Lilith or Mother Goddess of Depravity?

## Status

Preliminary planning notes from Planter/Mother, Moon, and Mother Goddess of Depravity taxonomy arbitration.

## Scope

### Endpoint Event

Reader-safe planning through the Book 1 ending and official EPUB artwork map.

### Investigation Window

Novel Chapters 1271, 1274, 1303, 1321, 1347, and 1378 were key checked anchors.

## Evidence

### Check 1: Outer Deity Layer

#### Chapter 1271

- What happened: Moon and Earth pathways are described as belonging to Mother Goddess of Depravity, and Primordial Moon is identified as that Outer Deity's infiltrating manifestation/pressure.
- Why it matters: Mother Goddess of Depravity should remain the `deity-od-` page for the active Outer Deity / Outer God entity.

#### Chapter 1378

- What happened: Primordial Moon is again equated with Mother Goddess of Depravity.
- Why it matters: Primordial Moon should be preserved as an alias/manifestation/corruption-facing name under Mother Goddess of Depravity, not split into a separate deity page at this stage.

### Check 2: Sequence 0 / True God Layer

#### Chapter 1274

- What happened: Earth Mother is identified with Lilith, who was once Sequence 0 of the Moon pathway.
- Why it matters: Earth Mother / Lilith should remain the `deity-s0-` page for the true god / holder identity layer.

#### Chapter 1303

- What happened: Sequence 0 of the Planter pathway is stated as Mother.
- Why it matters: Mother is a pathway endpoint/formal title and should not replace Planter as the pathway slug or Mother Goddess of Depravity as the Outer Deity page.

### Check 3: ATS / Great Old One Title Cluster

#### Chapter 1347

- What happened: The Great Old One title cluster is given as `Goddess of Origins, Mother of All, Brood Hive of Filth`, corresponding to `Brood Hive + Mother Uniqueness + The Moon Uniqueness + 1 Naturewalker characteristic + 1 Beauty Goddess characteristic`.
- Why it matters: This warrants a planned `deity-ats-goddess-of-origins.md` page for the Above the Sequences / Great Old One title cluster. `Mother Uniqueness` and `The Moon Uniqueness` should be tracked on their pathway pages unless later evidence makes either one a distinct named/embodied Uniqueness page.

## Conclusion

Create `deity-ats-goddess-of-origins.md` as the ATS / Great Old One title-cluster page for the Brood Hive + Mother + Moon configuration.

Keep it distinct from:

- `deity-od-mother-goddess-of-depravity.md`, the active Outer Deity / pillar identity.
- `deity-s0-earth-mother-lilith.md`, the Sequence 0 / true god / holder identity layer.

Use `Goddess of Origins` as the slug and display title because Chapter 1347 gives that exact plural form in the title cluster. Treat `Goddess of Origin` singular as a substring or artwork-label/translation variant unless later evidence supports it separately.

## Alias And Title Notes

- `Mother of All` should be preserved as an ATS title alias, but not used as the slug because it also appears earlier as Earth Mother worship language.
- `Brood Hive of Filth` should be preserved as an ATS title alias and tied to the `concept-brood-hive.md` / sefirah planning if that page is later created.
- `Primordial Moon` belongs primarily with the Mother Goddess of Depravity OD page as manifestation/corruption-facing terminology.
- `Mother Uniqueness` and `The Moon Uniqueness` are formula-component names, not currently distinct item/entity page targets.
